📋 1. Data We Collect

Veloce is designed with a privacy-first mindset. The information we handle falls into two categories:

Type Examples Where It Lives
Expense records Title, amount, date, category On-device only
Budget settings Category budgets, spending goals On-device only

⚙️ 2. How We Use Your Data

Data collected is used exclusively to provide and improve the Veloce experience:

  • Expense tracking — store, display, and summarise your spending across categories and time periods.
  • Budget management — calculate remaining budgets, progress ratios, and over-budget alerts.
  • AI expense parsing — convert natural-language text (e.g. "lunch 80k") into structured expense entries via the OpenAI API.
  • Subscription verification — validate your Pro entitlement through Apple's StoreKit to unlock premium features.
  • Usage limits — track the number of AI messages used today (stored locally) to enforce the free-tier limit of 3 messages per day.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core app functionality.

🤖 3. AI & Speech Processing

AI (OpenAI API)

When you use the AI assistant or natural-language input, the text of your message is sent to OpenAI's API to parse expense details. This data is transmitted over HTTPS and is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not instruct OpenAI to train on your inputs; requests are sent with the data-minimisation parameters available under their API terms.

Speech Recognition (Apple)

Voice input is processed using Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework (SFSpeechRecognizer). Audio is transcribed locally on your device before any text is handled. We never record, buffer, or upload raw audio to our own servers.

The AI receives only the expense text you type or speak — never your stored expense history, budget amounts, or personal details.

💾 4. Data Storage & Security

All expense records, categories, and budgets are stored as JSON files inside your iPhone's sandboxed Documents directory (veloce_categories.json, veloce_expenses.json). These files are:

  • Private — inaccessible to other apps due to iOS sandboxing.
  • Included in iCloud Backup — if you have iCloud Backup enabled, these files may be included in your personal backup, which is encrypted and controlled by your Apple ID.
  • Not synced to our servers — Veloce has no backend or cloud database of its own.

Write operations are dispatched to a background serial queue to protect data integrity. We strongly recommend enabling a device passcode and Face ID to protect your financial data.

🔗 5. Third-Party Services

Veloce integrates with the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • OpenAI API — AI expense parsing. Text queries only. openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Apple StoreKit 2 — in-app purchase validation for Pro subscriptions. Transaction data is managed by Apple. apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Apple Speech Framework — on-device speech-to-text for voice input.

We do not use analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, advertising networks, or social login providers.
